Augustus Pablo

In his teens, Mala discovered the music of Augustus Pablo, the Jamaican melodica player and producer. Pablo’s subtle melodic flourishes and experimental approach to production deeply influenced Mala’s work.

Pablo’s innovations included early dub production, collaborations with King Tubby and Lee “Scratch” Perry, and the integration of drum machines with organic instrumentation.

Born Horace Swaby in 1954, Pablo’s career spanned from the mid-70s until his death in 1999, with the late 70s period widely regarded as his finest.
