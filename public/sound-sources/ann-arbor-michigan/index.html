Ann Arbor, Michigan

Growing up in Ann Arbor, Michigan has had a lasting influence on Zach Shigeto’s musical output.

The city appears frequently in the titles of his productions, and the landscapes of his hometown regularly feature in his photography, particularly on Instagram. Ann Arbor’s cultural environment — home to the University of Michigan and a strong jazz heritage — played a formative role in shaping his early creative identity.
