Stones Throw Records

After graduating from university, Paul moved back to London and began teaching music production at CM Sounds. During this period he became deeply influenced by the work of Madlib, whose releases on Stones Throw Records were a major inspiration.

Founded by Peanut Butter Wolf, Stones Throw developed a distinctive catalogue rooted in hip-hop, soul, jazz, and experimentation. The 2013 documentary Our Vinyl Weighs a Ton chronicles the label’s history and cultural impact.

Madlib’s 2013 Medicine Show appearance in London further reinforced the label’s importance to Paul’s artistic outlook.
