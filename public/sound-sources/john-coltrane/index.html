John Coltrane

John Coltrane was a groundbreaking jazz saxophonist and composer whose innovative work reshaped the landscape of modern jazz. Renowned for his intense improvisations, spiritual depth, and technical mastery, Coltrane pushed musical boundaries with albums like Giant Steps and A Love Supreme. His relentless pursuit of musical exploration influenced generations of musicians and solidified his legacy as one of the most important figures in 20th-century music.
