Brian Blade — Fellowship

Shigeto moved east to attend college at The New School in New York, where he continued developing as a jazz drummer.

During this time, one of his biggest influences was Brian Blade, particularly Blade’s 1998 album Fellowship and the track Folklore. Blade’s expressive, musical approach to drumming — prioritising feel, restraint, and emotional communication — had a profound impact on Shigeto’s playing.

Brian Blade continues to lead the Fellowship while also collaborating with artists such as Joni Mitchell, Bob Dylan, and Wayne Shorter.
