Mo Kolours

Paul has long shared an affinity with the South London label 22a, and for good reason — he has been close friends with members of the collective for over a decade.

One of those artists is Joe ‘Mo Kolours’, whose work Paul has greatly admired. Over the years, both producers have encouraged and motivated each other creatively, developing parallel approaches rooted in groove, texture, and intuition.
