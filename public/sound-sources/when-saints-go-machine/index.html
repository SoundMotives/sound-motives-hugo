When Saints Go Machine

In 2007, Kenton Slash Demon joined forces with Simon Muschinsky and Nikolaj Manuel Vonsild to form the electro-pop band When Saints Go Machine.

Their later album Infinity Pool blends electronic pop with emotional songwriting, drawing comparisons to Tears for Fears and Kanye West’s 808s & Heartbreak**, and features a guest appearance from Killer Mike.

Their performance at Roskilde Festival in 2013 marked a homecoming moment, with the festival having played an important role in their musical development.
