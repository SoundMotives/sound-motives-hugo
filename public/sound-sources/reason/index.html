Reason

After leaving college and moving to London, Shigeto developed tendonitis, preventing him from playing drums for several months. The injury led to a period of depression, but also marked a turning point.

Encouraged by his brother, he began experimenting with music production instead of performance. Working with sequencers — particularly Reason — opened up an entirely new creative pathway, ultimately leading to his debut New Crossings EP in 2008.

This shift from physical performance to digital production reshaped Shigeto’s relationship with music and laid the foundation for his later work.
