Stormzy

Looking to the future, Mala acknowledges the next generation of UK artists, particularly Stormzy, a grime MC from South London/Croydon.

Stormzy’s self-released freestyles and tracks, including becoming the first unsigned MC to perform on Jools Holland, demonstrate the possibilities of innovation, longevity, and cultural reinvention — qualities Mala values in emerging artists.
