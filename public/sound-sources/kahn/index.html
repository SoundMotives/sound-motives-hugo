Kahn

Mala cites contemporary Bristol producer Kahn (Joseph McGann) as a current influence. Kahn’s work spans dubstep, grime, and reggae/digital styles through projects including Kahn & Neek, Bandulu Records, and Gorgon Sound, and he is part of the Young Echo Collective.

Kahn exemplifies Mala’s interest in artists who blend historical knowledge of bass music with fresh, authentic production.
