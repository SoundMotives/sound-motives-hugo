Jamie Woon

Earlier in his career, Paul’s creative process was largely solitary — producing beats remotely for MCs. More recently, he began collaborating directly with musicians in shared studio spaces.

One of those early face-to-face collaborations was with Jamie Woon on the track Thunder from Woon’s 2015 album Making Time.

These sessions helped Paul develop new approaches to songwriting, lyrics, and collective creativity, highlighting the power of working closely with others.
