Episode 03 — Mala

In this conversation, Mala — DJ, producer, promoter, and co‑founder of Digital Mystikz — explores his early influences from jungle and dub to club culture, discussing the evolution of dubstep, the role of Deep Medi, and how his sound has developed over time.
