Having experienced the power of combining sound with visuals early in his career, Max has frequently collaborated with visual artists.

One such collaborator is **Kevin McGloughlin**, a visual artist based in Sligo. McGloughlin’s extensive body of work explores light, motion, and abstraction, complementing Max’s interest in immersive audiovisual experiences.

Their collaborations reflect a shared interest in blurring the boundaries between music, film, and installation.
