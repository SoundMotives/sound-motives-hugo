**Tartelet Records** is a Copenhagen-based label originally run by Emil Margetli Nyholm and Frederik Bille Brahe. It was at Tartelet that **Kenton Slash Demon** developed their distinctive sound.

Over the years, the label has released influential music from artists such as **Brandt Brauer Frick**, **Max Graef**, and **Glenn Astro**, establishing itself as a hub for forward-thinking electronic music.

Tartelet’s emphasis on atmosphere, restraint, and long-form development played a significant role in shaping Kenton Slash Demon’s early releases.
